About Me

Sports

The reason I quit is because I had a back injury that prohibited me from doing most of the events that I competed on. My last goal was to make it to state, which I did, and then I would put down my grips and leave. Happily I still am involved with the gymnstics center because I work there as a coach for recreation kids. The sport that I do now sort of is equistrian vaulting which is basically gymnastics on a moving horse, I know it sounds crazy but if you look to the right you can see a picture of me doing a handstand on the horse. It is a really unique sport and I love doing it.
